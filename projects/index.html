Software

JuliaPOMDP

I am one of the core developers of JuliaPOMDP. The framework is a collection of algorithms and support tools for working with and solving Markov decision processes and their partially observable counterparts. It is written in Julia, and is aimed at both novices and experts in probabilistic planning and reinfrocement learning. With this framework, we aimed to make it simple to define a problem of interest and use one of the many solvers in our framework to compute a solution.
